How it works

From sidewall to shop reply in three clean moves.

The interface handles the details. The counter handles pricing, stock, installation terms, and timing.

1. Read the tire.

Find the size on the sidewall and add the vehicle. Complete inputs prevent avoidable back-and-forth.

2. Route the request.

Choose Redford or Detroit. Your phone opens a complete SMS draft to that counter.

3. Get the real number.

A team member replies with actual pricing and availability. No website estimate stands in for the shop.
